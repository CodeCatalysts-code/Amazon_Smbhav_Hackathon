Our Features & Services

Document Validator

A tool that verifies the accuracy and compliance of export-related documents, ensuring that all legal and regulatory requirements are met before shipping. This reduces the risk of delays and penalties.

Rate Comparator

Allows users to compare shipment rates across different carriers and select the most cost-effective and reliable option. This feature will be integrated using real-time APIs like the Shippo API.

Query Bot

An AI-powered chatbot designed to help users resolve their queries about shipping, customs, or other export-related issues in multiple languages, making it accessible to a wider range of users.

Shipment Tracker Map

A tool that provides real-time tracking of shipments, visually displaying their journey on an interactive map. It helps users monitor the progress of their shipments, ensuring timely delivery and offering transparency into potential delays or issues, improving operational efficiency.
